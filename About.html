Welcome to Teemolly!
Teemolly website belongs to a Teemolly project. Established on September 7, 2016, Teemolly's original goal was to provide users with tailor-made, personalized designs. Our website is the place to showcase the design images, we are the design service
So, how does it work?
1. Tee Molly will transfer the design file for the Platform.
What is Platform ?
- The platform is the site of the T-shirt printing facility, where it will receive design files, print shirts and shipping it to you.
We chose the platform for criteria:
- Lowest shipping cost.
- Service as well as a good quality shirt.
- Customer support hearted.
- Can return the shirt if you do not like.
2. You look at the design and choose the buy link, (buy link we get from the platform).
You can recommend the design on demand (pictures you like, say you want...)
3. You purchase the shirt based on the link we provide; the Platform will accept your request, you will buy it and receive the shirt.
